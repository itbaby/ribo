## Technology Stack
- **Framework**: Svelte 5
- **Build Tool**: Vite
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS
- **Routing**: Hash-based routing
- **Animations**: GSAP, AOS (Animate On Scroll)
- **Scrolling**: Locomotive Scroll
- **Internationalization**: Svelte-i18n
- **UI Components**: Flowbite Svelte
- **Icons**: Remix Icons, Flag Icons

## Key Features
1. **Multi-language Support** (English, Chinese, Japanese)
2. **Responsive Design** with mobile navigation
3. **Animated Scrolling** and section transitions
4. **Component-based Architecture** with reusable UI elements
5. **Industry-specific Pages** for different service offerings
6. **Client Showcase** with sponsor logos
7. **News & Updates** section
8. **Contact Form** with validation
9. **Company Information** pages (About, Culture, History, etc.)

## Routing Structure
- `#/` - Home Page (CDefault)
- `#/industry` - Industry Solutions (CIndustry)
- `#/hiring` - Headhunting Services (CHiring)
- `#/resources` - Staffing Services (CResources)
- `#/outsourcing` - IT Outsourcing (COutSourcing)
- `#/aboutus` - About Us (CAboutUs)
- `#/contact` - Contact Form (CContact)
- `#/innovation` - Innovation (CInnovation)
- `#/intro` - Company Introduction (CIntro)
- `#/culture` - Company Culture (CCulture)
- `#/honors` - Awards & Recognition (CHonors)
- `#/info` - Company Details (CInfo)

## Data Flow
1. **Entry Point** (main.ts) initializes:
   - Internationalization with locale files
   - AOS animations
   - Flowbite components
   - Mounts App.svelte

2. **App.svelte** handles:
   - Hash-based routing
   - Component rendering based on route
   - Menu and navigation state

3. **Components** utilize:
   - i18n for translated content
   - GSAP for complex animations
   - AOS for scroll-triggered animations
   - Tailwind for responsive styling
